# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insults, or deliberate disruption
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, and issues that violate this policy.

## Scope

This policy applies in project spaces and in public spaces when someone is representing this project.
